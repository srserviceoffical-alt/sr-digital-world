Frequently Asked Questions

Whatever your question is, the answer might already be here.

How to place an order?Order kaise place karein?
Go to the Products or Pricing page, select your desired tool and duration, click "Order Now", enter your name and WhatsApp number — the message will automatically be sent to our WhatsApp.
Products ya Pricing page par jayein, apna desired tool aur duration select karein, "Order Now" click karein, naam aur WhatsApp number dalein — message automatically WhatsApp par chala jayega.
How long does delivery take?Delivery kitni dair mein hoti hai?
Once the payment is confirmed, account credentials are usually sent via WhatsApp within 10-30 minutes. It might take a little longer during peak hours.
Payment confirm hote hi, usually 10-30 minutes ke andar account credentials WhatsApp par bhej diye jaate hain. Peak hours mein thoda time lag sakta hai.
How can I track my order?Order track kaise karein?
After placing an order, WhatsApp is our single point of contact — you will receive all updates in that same chat.
Order place karne ke baad WhatsApp hi single point of contact hai — wahi chat mein updates milte rehte hain.
Can an order be canceled?Kya order cancel ho sakta hai?
Any order can be canceled for free before payment. After payment, cancellation must be discussed on WhatsApp.
Payment se pehle koi bhi order free mein cancel ho sakta hai. Payment ke baad cancellation WhatsApp par discuss karni hogi.
Which payment methods are accepted?Payment ke kaunse methods accept hote hain?
We accept JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and Bank Transfers. You will receive payment details on WhatsApp after confirming your order.
JazzCash, EasyPaisa aur Bank Transfer — sab accept hote hain. Order confirm hone ke baad WhatsApp par payment details mil jaati hain.
Is advance payment required?Kya advance payment zaroori hai?
Yes, payment needs to be confirmed before delivery so that order processing can begin immediately.
Haan, delivery se pehle payment confirm karni hoti hai taake order processing turant shuru ho sake.
Do you offer refunds?Kya refund milta hai?
If there is a genuine issue with account access, we process a replacement or refund within 24 hours.
Agar account access mein koi genuine issue ho toh hum 24 ghante ke andar replace ya refund process karte hain.
Are these subscriptions genuine or shared?Yeh subscriptions genuine hain ya shared?
All subscriptions are verified and original — we do not provide any fake or cracked versions.
Sab subscriptions verified aur original hain — koi fake ya cracked versions nahi diye jaate.
What happens after the subscription expires?Subscription expire hone ke baad kya hoga?
You can contact us on WhatsApp for renewal — you will need to place a new order with your preferred duration.
Aap WhatsApp par renewal ke liye contact kar sakte hain — naya order place karna hoga apne preferred duration ke saath.
Can I get a tool that is not on the list?Kya koi aur tool bhi mil sakta hai jo list mein nahi hai?
Message us on WhatsApp with your requirement — if it's available, we will definitely provide it.
WhatsApp par message karein apni requirement ke saath — agar available hua toh hum zaroor provide karenge.
When is support available?Support kab available hota hai?
24/7 — whether it's day or night, send us a message on WhatsApp and you'll usually get a reply within 30 minutes.
24/7 — raat ho ya din, WhatsApp par message bhejein aur usually 30 minutes ke andar reply milta hai.
What should I do if there's a problem with my account?Agar account mein problem aa jaye toh kya karein?
Message us immediately on WhatsApp with your order details — we will resolve it or replace the account as soon as possible.
Turant WhatsApp par order details ke saath message karein — hum jald se jald resolve karenge ya replace kar denge.
